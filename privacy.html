Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Audio Visualizer ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Song Information: When you grant notification access, we detect the currently playing song title and artist to look up BPM data. This is processed in real-time and not stored on your device or our servers.
  • BPM Lookups: Song titles and artists are sent to our server to retrieve BPM data. We cache BPM values (not linked to you) to improve lookup speed for all users.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, anonymous diagnostic information is sent to help us fix bugs.
  • Basic Analytics: We collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app opens, features used) to improve the app.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We don't access your music files or playlists
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't sell any data to third parties

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: For crash reporting and analytics (Firebase Privacy)
  • Supabase: For BPM data storage (Supabase Privacy)
  • GetSongBPM: For BPM lookups (GetSongBPM Privacy)
  • Google Play: For purchases (Google Privacy)

Permissions

  • Notification Access: Required to detect currently playing songs. You can revoke this anytime in Android Settings.
  • Network Access: Required for BPM lookups and Chromecast connectivity.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on our GitHub or reach out through the in-app feedback form.
